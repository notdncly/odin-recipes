Caesar Salad

Description

a tossed salad usually made of romaine, garlic, anchovies, and croutons and dressed with olive oil, coddled egg, lemon juice, and grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. Make croutons
  2. Make Caesar Salad dressing
  3. Combine all the ingredients
